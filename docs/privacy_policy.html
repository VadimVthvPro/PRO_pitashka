1. INTRODUCTION

Welcome to PROpitashka Bot! We take the protection of your personal data and privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

2. DATA COLLECTED

We collect and process the following information:

  • Telegram ID and username: To identify you in our system
  • Health data: Height, weight, age, gender - to calculate personalized recommendations
  • Fitness and nutrition goals: To provide tailored meal and training plans
  • Training and nutrition history: To track your progress
  • Food photos: If you choose to send them for AI analysis
  • Messages and requests: To improve our service quality
  • UTM parameters and referral codes: To understand traffic sources

3. DATA USAGE

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Providing personalized meal and training plans
  • Tracking progress and achieving your goals
  • Calculating calories and nutritional value using AI
  • Generating AI-powered recommendations (via Google Gemini)
  • Improving service quality and user experience
  • Analytics and marketing attribution

3.1. AI Processing

We use Google Gemini AI to generate personalized recommendations. Your requests are processed according to Google's Privacy Policy. We cache AI responses using Redis to improve performance - cached data is automatically deleted after 24 hours.

4. DATA STORAGE AND PROTECTION

  • Secure servers: All data is stored on PostgreSQL databases with encryption
  • Encryption in transit: SSL/TLS encryption protects data transmission
  • Access control: Only authorized systems have access to your data
  • No third-party sharing: We never share your data with third parties without your explicit consent
  • Automatic backups: Daily backups ensure data safety

5. YOUR RIGHTS (GDPR/CCPA)

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of all your data
  • Right to Deletion: Request complete deletion of your account and data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Right to Portability: Receive your data in machine-readable format
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Stop data processing at any time
  • Right to Object: Object to certain types of processing

To exercise your rights, contact us via the bot or email.

6. DATA RETENTION

  • Active accounts: Data is stored as long as your account is active
  • Inactive accounts: After 12 months of inactivity, we may delete your data
  • Deleted accounts: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Legal obligations: Some data may be retained longer to comply with legal requirements

7. COOKIES AND CACHING

We use Redis for caching AI responses to improve performance. The cache is automatically cleared after:

  • AI responses: 1 hour
  • Recipes and meal plans: 24 hours
  • User session data: 7 days

8. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

9. POLICY CHANGES

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be announced through the bot. Your continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

10. CONTACT

For questions, concerns, or to exercise your rights:

  • Via bot: Use the /support command
  • Email: privacy@propitashka.com
  • Response time: Within 72 hours
